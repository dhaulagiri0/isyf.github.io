About this year's theme

In 2021, ISYF will be conducted fully online. Our ISYFdigital 2021 theme, “Post-pandemic science: navigating uncharted waters”, hopes to bring to light the evolving role of science in the new normal we are currently living in.

Now, more than ever, we are dependent on scientific innovations to help us address the new challenges brought about by the COVID 19 pandemic. From grappling with social distancing policies such as international border closures, lockdowns, quarantine and working from home to racing to find an effective and safe vaccine, science must continue to evolve, clear roadblocks and lift us out of this crisis.

As we look to science for answers, let us consider some pertinent questions. In what ways has the pandemic affected the typical research scientist? How have their day-to-day lives been changed? What areas of research are more important or emerging now, what others have become less relevant? Does science have a role in educating the general public about controversial news surrounding the pandemic?
