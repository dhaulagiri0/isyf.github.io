Welcome

About ISYF

The International Science Youth Forum (ISYFdigital) is a prestigious science enrichment programme for top research students from all over the globe. It is hosted online by Hwa Chong Institution (HCI), under the Students' Science Research Club (SSRC). Every year, delegates get to benefit from participating in a multitude of fun and meaningful events, from bonding activities, to learning about different cultures and having a rare opportunity to ask questions to Nobel Laureates. This year however, due to the COVID-19 pandemic, we have adapted our programme to a virtual event.

The theme for ISYFdigital 2021 is “Post-Pandemic Science, Navigating Uncharted Waters”, which highlights the evolving role of science in the new normal that we are currently living in. From quarantines to border closures, global research has now converged towards the kingpin of finding a cure to COVID-19, sparking pretinent questions to the future of scientific research. In what ways has the pandemic affected scientific progress? After this tumultuous journey ends, what will the world be like?

ISYFdigital 2021 hopes to be the vessel that brings its delegates to overcome these murky waters, and propel them in the direction of a hopeful future.

Co-Chairpersons’ Welcome

Dear Student Delegates,

Welcome to the 13th International Science Youth Forum Digital (ISYFdigital), held from 19th January to 22nd January 2020!

These adverse times make change imperative. And it is only through exploring the unknown that we can press through these tough times. This leads us to our theme for ISYFdigital 2021 - Post-Pandemic Science, Navigating Uncharted Waters. It stresses the importance of Science as a beacon of guiding light that navigates us through these uncertain times.

Despite going digital, ISYF continues to strive for perfection to inspire inquisitive minds. Intellectual discussions with eminent scientists can broaden your horizons and encourage you to explore new concepts. Through collaboration and innovation, you can look forward to meeting like-minded individuals to further your passion.

The Organising Team of the 13th ISYFdigital wishes that you will have a memorable and enriching experience!

Warmest regards,
Tan Jia Shuen, Maegan and Teng Jia Jun, Zen
Co-Chairpersons
ISYFdigital 2021 Student Organising Committee
