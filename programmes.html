Programme Details

Things to look forward to!

Meet-the-Scientist

During Meet-the-Scientist Sessions, delegates can look forward to an exciting and interactive time with the scientists to discover more about their lives and their passion for Science. Delegates will have the opportunity to pose their burning questions to the scientist, and learn more about what a career in Science entails.

At the ISYF Keynote address, delegates will hear from a Nobel Laureate who will share his research experience and views on issues centered around the theme of the forum, as well as address questions posed by the audience. The panel discussion and question-and-answer session will be facilitated by panel moderators. Delegates can look forward to glimpse into the life of a scientist through the Nobel Laureate’s sharing of his insights.

Team Project Challenge

As ISYF2021 migrates to an online platform, Team Project Challenge, an activity that has traditionally been present in past ISYF events, will have to adapt to the new virtual environment. From a conventionally physical activity, TPC this year has to function within the constraints of a virtual set-up. However, this has allowed TPC to take on new features never seen before, and undergo a revamp in structure. Hence, the TPC of ISYF2021 will be novel to many while retaining the spirit of teamwork and science as a common language.

TPC will work to engage delegates across various countries to work together on a common goal. This process will involve research, collaboration, brainstorming and ideation, all of which will seek to bring delegates together even when they are miles away from each other physically. Science is of particular importance and pertinence in this day and age, and TPC expounds on this relevance to have delegates engage in these conversations and effective discourse to allow them to walk away with more passion and knowledge with respect to science.

Poster Exhibition

While many of us come from different delegations and backgrounds, we are all connected through our passions for science. The research poster exhibition serves to allow delegates to share their different experiences in research from the different aspects of science. We can all stand to learn something from the research experience of another, and the exhibition allows for that to happen.

The poster showcase will then feature the top projects among those presented, and these groups will have the opportunity to take their project a step further by presenting them live in front of everyone as well as guest judges! Winners will be announced and given exclusive prizes as a celebration of their achievements.

Cultural Exhibition

While we converge virtually to have meaningful discussions on science, delegates also come from different backgrounds and cultures. Beyond having these discussions on science, ISYFdigital 2021 is also a celebration of how delegates are able to come together despite their cultural differences and have meaningful interactions with each other. It is a reminder that these differences between our cultures and heritage, may it be minute or significant, should be celebrated rather than rejected.

What other way to celebrate these differences than to share them with everyone else? Hence, our cultural exhibition and sharings is a platform where we get to learn and appreciate each other’s cultures first hand. It allows us to to immerse ourselves in cultures of other delegations and understand them better too. This is to ultimately enrich delegates in the various cultures of the different delegates that have gathered virtually for ISYFdigital 2021.
